About Us

Red Gables @ Wolf Branch started as an idea in the fall of 2023 when we saw the farm on the Cumberland Plateau, Tennessee, that would become ours in 2024. Construction began at the farm in spring of 2024 and continues to this day. The cabin already on the property will be remodeled and will become the first cabin at the Red Gables @ Wolf Branch Airbnb. Along with being an Airbnb, the farm will be available for different events. These events will be hosted by different groups, such as local homeschool groups, and will include dances, bonfires, cookouts, book/coffee club meetings, and holiday parties.
